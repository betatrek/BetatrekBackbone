Description

Australia Acquisition Corp. is a blank check company. The Company is a development-stage company. The Company was formed for the purpose of acquiring or acquiring control of one or more operating businesses or assets that it has not identified through a merger, capital stock exchange, asset or stock acquisition, exchangeable share transaction or other similar business transaction. The Company focuses on operating businesses that have their primary operations located in the Commonwealth of Australia. During the year ended June 30, 2011, the Company did not generate any revenues. As of July 31, 2010, the Company had not commenced any operations.
