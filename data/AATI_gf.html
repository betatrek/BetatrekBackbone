Description

Advanced Analogic Technologies Incorporated (AATI) is engaged in the design, development, marketing and sale of power management semiconductor products and solutions for the communications, computing and consumer portable and personal electronics marketplace. It addresses the application-specific needs of consumer, communications and computing electronic devices, such as wireless handsets, notebook and tablet computers, camera phones, digital cameras, personal media players, Bluetooth headphones and accessories, digital television, set top boxes and displays. Through its Total Power Management approach, AATI offers a range of products that support multiple applications, features, and services across a varied set of electronic devices. It offers a portfolio of approximately 800 power management products comprising power management application-specific standard products, and selected general-purpose analog integrated circuits, in single-chip, multi-chip and chip-scale packages.
