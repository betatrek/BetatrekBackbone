Description

Anchor Bancorp Wisconsin Inc. is a savings and loan holding company. The Company is engaged in the savings and loan business through its wholly owned banking subsidiary, AnchorBank, fsb (the Bank). The Bank offers checking, savings, money market accounts, mortgages, home equity and other consumer loans, credit cards, annuities, investment products and related consumer financial services. The Bank also provides banking services to businesses, including checking accounts, lines of credit, secured loans and commercial real estate loans. The Bank has two wholly owned subsidiaries: ADPC Corporation, which holds and develops certain of the Bank�s foreclosed properties, and Anchor Investment Corporation, an operating subsidiary that manages a portion of the Bank�s investment portfolio (primarily mortgage-related securities). On July 23, 2010, the Company completed the sale of four branches located in Green Bay, Wisconsin.
