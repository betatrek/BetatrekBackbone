Description

Autobytel Inc. (Autobytel), incorporated on May 17, 1996, is an automotive marketing services company that assists automotive retail dealers (Dealers) and automotive manufacturers (Manufacturers) market and sells new and used vehicles through its Internet purchase request referral programs, together with related Dealer marketing products and services, and online advertising programs. Autobytelï¿½s network of owned, consumer-facing automotive Websites (Owned Websites), which include Autobytel.com, Autotropolis.com, Autoweb.com, AutoSite.com, Car.comsm, CarSmart.com, CarTV.com, MyGarage.com and MyRide.com, provide consumers with information and tools to aid them with their automotive purchase decisions and the opportunity to submit Purchase Requests. On September 17, 2010, the Company acquired all of the assets of two privately-held companies, Autotropolis, Inc. and Cyber Ventures, Inc. (Auto/Cyber).
