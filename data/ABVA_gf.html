Description

Alliance Bankshares Corporation (Bankshares) is a bank holding company. The Company conducts substantially all of its operations through its subsidiaries. Its banking subsidiary, Alliance Bank Corporation (the Bank), is a state-chartered commercial bank. The Bank focuses on serving the needs of individuals, small and medium size businesses and professional concerns in the greater Washington, D.C. Metropolitan region. It uses the Internet to offer online account access, bill payment and commercial cash management. In addition, certain loan and deposit products may be offered from time to time on its Website, as well as at its physical locations. As of December 31, 2010, the Bank had six full-service banking facilities. In addition to the Bank, Bankshares has another subsidiary, Alliance Virginia Capital Trust I (Trust), a Delaware statutory trust. The Bank conducts its business via two key business lines: Commercial Banking and Retail Banking.
