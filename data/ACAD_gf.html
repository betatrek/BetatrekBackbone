Description

ACADIA Pharmaceuticals Inc. is a biopharmaceutical company focused on the development and commercialization of small molecule drugs for the treatment of central nervous system disorders. Its pipeline consists of four product candidates including pimavanserin, which is in Phase-III development as a treatment for Parkinson�s disease psychosis. It has global commercialization rights to pimavanserin. In addition, it has a product candidate in Phase II development for chronic pain and a product candidate in Phase I development for glaucoma, both in collaboration with Allergan, Inc., as well as a program in IND-track development in collaboration with Meiji Seika Kaisha, Ltd. Its product candidates include Pimavanserin, AGN-XX/YY, AC-262271 and AM-831.
