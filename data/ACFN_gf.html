Description

Acorn Energy, Inc. is a holding company, which provides solutions for energy infrastructure asset management. The Company operates in five segments: Air Pollution Control Services, Energy and Security Sonar Solutions, Smart Grid Distribution Automation and Energy, Security Sensor Systems and Other. On February 23, 2010, the Company acquired US Sensor Systems Inc. (USSI). On April 30, 2010 the Company, through its subsidiary acquired Decision Dynamics Technology Ltd. On May 12, 2010, the Company acquired GridSense. In addition, On May 20, 2010, the Company through its subsidiary, GridSense acquired On-Line Monitoring Inc. (OMI). In September 2010, it increased the ownership of US Sensor Systems Inc. to 51%. In December 2010, it sold Coreworx Inc. In April 2011, the Company sold its 25% interest in HangXing International Automation Engineering Co. Ltd. (HangXing). In August 2011, the Company sold CoaLogix, Inc.
