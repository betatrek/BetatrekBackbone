Description

Axcelis Technologies, Inc. (Axcelis) designs, manufactures and services ion implantation, dry strip and other processing equipment used in the fabrication of semiconductor chips. The Company sells to semiconductor chip manufacturers worldwide. The ion implantation business comprised approximately 74.5% of its revenue in during the year ended December 31, 2011, with the remaining 25.5% of revenue derived from its dry strip and other processing systems. In addition to equipment, Axcelis provides aftermarket service and support, including spare parts, equipment upgrades, maintenance services and customer training.
