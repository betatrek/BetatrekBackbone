Description

ACNB Corporation (ACNB) is a financial holding company. The Company, through its banking and non-banking subsidiaries, provides a range of banking and financial services to individuals and businesses, including commercial and retail banking, trust and investment management, and insurance. During the year ended December 31, 2010, the Company�s banking operations were conducted through its operating subsidiary, Adams County National Bank (ACNB Bank), with 19 retail banking offices in Adams, Cumberland and York Counties, as well as two loan production offices in York and Franklin Counties, Pennsylvania. The Company�s Non-Banking Subsidiaries include Russell Insurance Group, Inc. (RIG) and BankersRe Insurance Group, SPC.
