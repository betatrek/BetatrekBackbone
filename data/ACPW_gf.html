Description

Active Power, Inc. (Active Power) is a manufacturer of uninterruptible power supply (UPS) systems, continuous power and infrastructure solutions. The solutions ensure continuity for business and Information Technology (IT) operations for enterprises, data center operations and IT service providers in the event of power disturbances. The UPS products the Company manufactures use kinetic energy to provide short-term power as a cleaner alternative to electro-chemical battery-based energy. The Companys products and services include CleanSource UPS and direct current (DC) Products; PowerHouse Systems; Continuous Infrastructure, and GenSTART. The Companys products and solutions are designed to deliver continuous clean power, during power disturbances and outages, voltage sags and surges and provide ride-through power in the event of utility failure supporting operations until utility power is restored or a longer term alternative power source is started.
