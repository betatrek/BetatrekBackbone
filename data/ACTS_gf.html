Description

Actions Semiconductor Co., Ltd. (Actions), incorporated on July 27, 2005, is a fabless semiconductor company that designs, develops and markets integrated platform solutions, including system-on-chips (SoCs), firmware, software development tools and reference designs, for manufacturers of portable media players. It provides integrated platform solutions to portable media player manufacturers, brand owners and distributors. Its integrated platform solutions incorporate the features, which include SoCs, firmware, software development tools and reference designs; third-party distributors and applications developers enable the customers to introduce products with differentiated features to customize the solutions. On August 31, 2010, the Company acquired additional interest in one of its equity method investees, Mavrix Technology, Inc. (Mavrix). The Company provides SoCs and solution development kits (SDKs).
