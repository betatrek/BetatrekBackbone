Description

Adept Technology, Inc. (Adept) is a global provider of intelligent robots and autonomous mobile solutions and services. The product range includes application software, integrated real-time vision and multi-axis motion controls, machine vision systems and software, autonomous navigation software and controls, industrial robots and grippers, autonomous service robots, intelligent automated guided vehicles (AGVs), and vision-based flexible parts feeders. The Companyï¿½s offering combines its motion controls systems with application software, which it generally sells together with its own vision-guidance technology and/or its robot mechanisms. The vision guidance technology is integrated with its motion controls technology. The Company operates in two segments: Robotics and Services and Support. On January 10, 2011, the Company acquired InMoTx, Inc. On June 25, 2010, Adept acquired MobileRobots, Inc.
