Description

Adams Golf, Inc. (Adams Golf), incorporated in 1987, designs, assembles, markets and distributes golf clubs for all skill levels. The Company�s products include Idea Tech V3 irons and hybrids, Idea a7 and a7 OS irons and hybrids, Speedline Fast 11 and Speedline 9064 drivers, Speedline Fast 11 fairway woods, and Idea Pro Black hybrids and irons. The Company also developed products under the names of Yes! Putters, Women's Golf Unlimited, the Lady Fairway and Square 2 brands. On January 20, 2011, the Company acquired certain assets of Progear Holdings, Inc, doing business as Yes! Golf.
