Description

BLDRS Asia 50 ADR Index Fund (the Fund) is an exchange-traded fund based on the BNY Mellon Asia 50 ADR Index (Index). The Fund normally holds at least 95% of its total assets in Depositary Receipts that comprise the Index. The Index is capitalization-weighted and designed to track the performance of approximately 50 Asian market-based depositary receipts. The Fund invests in sectors, such as consumer discretionary, energy, financials, healthcare, industrials, information technology, materials, telecommunication services and utilities. The sponsor of the BLDRS Index Funds Trust (BLDRS), a unit investment trust, is Invesco PowerShares Capital Management LLC (Invesco PowerShares).
