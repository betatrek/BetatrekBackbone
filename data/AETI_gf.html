Description

American Electric Technologies, Inc. (AETI) is a global provider of power delivery solutions to the renewable energy industries. As of December 31, 2010, AETI�s corporate structure consists of American Electric Technologies, Inc., which owns 100% of both M&I Electric Industries, Inc. (M&I) and American Access Technologies, Inc. (AAT). The Company operates in three segments: Technical Products and Services (TP&S), Electrical and Instrumentation Construction (E&I), and American Access (AAT). TP&S segment and E&I segment, which together encompass the operations of M&I, including its South Coast Electric Systems, LLC subsidiary. Its AAT segment encompasses the operations of American Access including its Omega Metals division. The Company offers power distribution and power conversion systems, power services, and electrical and instrumentation construction. In March 2012, the Company acquired the technology of Amnor Technologies, Inc. (Amnortech) of Shepherd, Texas.
