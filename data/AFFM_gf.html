Description

Affirmative Insurance Holdings, Inc. (Affirmative) is a distributor and producer of non-standard personal automobile insurance policies and related products and services for individual consumers. As of December 31, 2010, the Companyï¿½s subsidiaries included insurance companies licensed to write policies in 40 states, underwriting agencies, and retail agencies with 202-owned stores and relationships with two unaffiliated underwriting agencies. The Company offers insurance directly to individual consumers through retail stores in nine states (Louisiana, Texas, Illinois, Alabama, Missouri, Indiana, South Carolina, Kansas and Wisconsin) and distributing its own insurance policies through 9,200 independent agents or brokers in 9 states (Louisiana, Texas, Illinois, Alabama, California, Michigan, Missouri, Indiana and South Carolina). In July 2010, the Company discontinued writing new and renewal insurance policies in New Mexico.
