Description

Alliance Fiber Optic Products Inc. (AFOP) designs, manufactures and markets a range of fiber optic components, and integrated modules incorporating these components, for communications equipment manufacturers and service providers. It also offers a range of products, including interconnect devices that are used to connect optical fibers and components, couplers and splitters that are used to divide and combine optical power, and dense wavelength division multiplexing (DWDM) devices that separate and combine multiple specific wavelengths. Alliance Fiber Optic Products is a subsidiary of the Company. The Company�s connectivity products include optical interconnect devices, couplers and splitters, planar lightwave circuits (PLC) and related optical products, as well as customized integrated modules incorporating these devices. Its optical passive products include wavelength division multiplexing (WDM), Coarse wavelength division multiplexers (CWDM) and DWDM components and modules
