Description

Amtrust Financial Services, Inc. (AmTrust) is a multinational specialty property and casualty insurer company, focused on generating consistent underwriting profits. The Company has operations in four business segments: Small Commercial Business, Specialty Risk and Extended Warranty, Specialty Program and Personal Lines Reinsurance. It transacts business primarily through 11 insurance company subsidiaries, which include Technology Insurance Company, Inc., Rochdale Insurance Company, Wesco Insurance Company, Associated Industries Insurance Company, Inc., Milwaukee Casualty Insurance Company, Security National Insurance Company, AmTrust Insurance Company of Kansas, Inc., AmTrust Lloyds Insurance Company, AmTrust International Underwriters Limited, AmTrust International Insurance Ltd and AmTrust Europe, Ltd. In January 2012, it acquired Builders & Tradesmen's Insurance Services, Inc. In February 2012, it purchased the renewal rights of BancInsure, Inc.'s in-force insurance policies.
