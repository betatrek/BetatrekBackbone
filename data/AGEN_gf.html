Description

Agenus Inc., (Agenus), formerly Antigenics Inc., is a biotechnology company focused on the development and commercialization of technologies to treat cancers and infectious diseases, based on immunological approaches. The Company�s products and Technologies include Heat Shock Protein Technology, The Prophage Series of Cancer Vaccines, Phase 3 Renal Cell Carcinoma Program, Glioma. The Company�s key assets include The Prophage Series of cancer vaccines, QS-21 Stimulon adjuvant (QS-21) and HerpV. The Prophage Series of cancer vaccines is based on its core heat shock protein technology. QS-21 is an adjuvant, or a substance added to a vaccine or other immunotherapy that is intended to enhance immune response. HerpV is a therapeutic vaccine for the treatment of genital herpes, which is based on the Company�s Heat shock proteins (HSP) technology.
