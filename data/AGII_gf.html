Description

Argo Group International Holdings, Ltd. (Argo Group) is an international underwriter of specialty insurance and reinsurance products in the property and casualty market. During the year ended December 31, 2011, the Company operated in four business segments: Excess and surplus lines carriers focus on risks that the standard (admitted) market is unwilling or unable to underwrite; Commercial Specialty segment provides property, casualty and surety coverages; International Specialty segment consists of Argo Group�s business outside the United States, and The Syndicate 1200 segment focuses on underwriting worldwide property, specialty and non-United States liability insurance on behalf of one underwriting syndicate. In September 2011, a new subsidiary, Argo Re (DIFC) Ltd. was launched in Dubai, the United Arab Emirates to serve the Middle East and North African insurance community. During 2011, it established Argo Seguros and ArgoGlobal SE in its International Specialty segment.
