Description

Agilysys, Inc. (Agilysys) is a provider of information technology (IT) solutions to corporate and public-sector customers, with special expertise in select markets, including retail and hospitality. Agilysys develops technology solutions, including hardware, software, and services to help customers resolve their IT data center and point-of-sale needs. The Company�s point-of-sale solutions include property management, inventory and procurement, point-of-sale, and document management software, services, including expertise in mobility and wireless solutions for retailers, and resold hardware, software, and services. A portion of the point-of-sale related revenue is recurring from software support and hardware maintenance agreements. Agilysys business segments include Hospitality Solutions Group (HSG) and Retail Solutions Group (RSG). In August 1, 2011, the Company completed its Technology Solutions Group (TSG) business unit.
