Description

Alanco Technologies, Inc. (Alanco) is a provider of information technology solutions. Its business operations focus is providing wireless monitoring and asset management solutions through its StarTrak Systems, LLC (StarTrak) subsidiary. StarTrak Systems is the provider of tracking, monitoring and control services to the refrigerated transportation marketplace. On February 28, 2010, the Company sold Excel/Meridian Data, Inc., its wholly owned subsidiary conducting Data Storage operation, to an entity controlled by an investment banking group located in Los Angeles, California. On August 18, 2010, the Company announced the divestiture of Alanco/TSI PRISM, Inc. (ATSI) operations with the sale of assets and business of ATSI to Black Creek Integrated Systems Corp.
