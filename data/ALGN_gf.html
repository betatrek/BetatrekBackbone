Description

Align Technology, Inc (Align) designs, manufactures and markets a system of clear aligner therapy, intra-oral scanners and computer-aided design and computer-aided manufacturing (CAD/CAM) digital services used in dentistry, orthodontics, and dental records storage. Align operates in two segments: Clear Aligner, known as the Invisalign system; and Scanners and CAD/CAM Services, known as iTero and iOC intra-oral scanners and OrthoCAD services. During the year ended December 31, 2011, Clear Aligner revenues represent approximately 94% of revenue, while Scanners and CAD/CAM Services represent the remaining 6% of revenues. The Company distributes the majority of its products directly to the customers include orthodontists and general practitioner dentists (GPs), as well as to restorative dentists, including prosthodontists, periodontists, and oral surgeons. On April 29, 2011, the Company acquired Cadent Holdings, Inc. (Cadent).
